About IJEM – In Jesus Ever Ministries

IJEM – In Jesus Ever Ministries is a Christ-centered ministry dedicated to prayer, discipleship, evangelism, and spiritual growth.

Our Vision

5 Billion souls Our vision is to nurture and empower young individuals, fostering their growth as devoted disciples who strive to manifest the heavenly kingdom on earth.

Our Mission

We provide guidance and support to help them uncover their life’s purpose, offering a range of opportunities, educational programs, counseling and individualized attention.

  • Consistent prayer and intercession
  • Teaching the Word of God
  • Evangelism and outreach
  • Discipling youth and children
