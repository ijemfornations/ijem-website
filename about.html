About IJEM – In Jesus Ever Ministries

IJEM – In Jesus Ever Ministries is a Christ-centered ministry dedicated to prayer, discipleship, evangelism, and spiritual growth.

Our Vision

5 Billion Souls
Our vision is to nurture and empower young individuals, fostering their growth as devoted disciples who strive to manifest the Kingdom of Heaven on earth.

Our Mission

We provide guidance and support to help individuals uncover their life’s purpose, offering spiritual training, discipleship, counseling, and personal mentorship.

  • Consistent prayer and intercession
  • Teaching and living the Word of God
  • Evangelism and outreach
  • Discipling youth and children
